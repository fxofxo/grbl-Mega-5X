LEDS.

LED1 is connected to the Arduino pin D13 and mirrors the Arduino 'power' LED
LED2 is connected to the Arduino pin D8 and shows the PWM output to the hot end (wiring at D8 terminals)
LED3 is connected to the Arduino pin D9 and shows the PWM output to the fan (wiring at D9 terminals)
LED4 is connected to the Arduino pin D10 and shows the PWM output to the heated bed (wiring at D10 terminals)
